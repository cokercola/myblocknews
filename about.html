About myblocknews

Last updated July 2026

myblocknews is a lightweight crypto markets and news dashboard. We pull live price data directly from CoinGecko's public market API and aggregate headlines from established crypto news outlets, so you can check prices and catch up on what's happening in one place without digging through a dozen tabs.

What we are

A simple, fast reference point. Live prices for major coins, refreshed automatically, alongside a running feed of headlines pulled from trusted third-party publications.

What we're not

We are not a financial advisory service, a broker, or an exchange. We don't hold funds, execute trades, or offer investment recommendations. Nothing on this site should be treated as financial advice — see our Privacy Policy and Terms for the full picture.

Sourcing

Market data comes from CoinGecko's public API. Headlines are aggregated from outlets including CoinDesk, Cointelegraph, and Decrypt, with attribution shown next to each headline. We link out to original sources rather than reproducing full articles.
