What three weeks of rising ETF inflows actually tells you, and what it doesn't

context on reporting from Reuters, CoinDesk

Inflows rising for three straight weeks is a real, verifiable data point. What it does not tell you is where the next three weeks go. Context matters more than the headline number alone.
