Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

This policy explains what information myblocknews collects when you visit the site and how it's used. We've kept it short because we don't collect much.

Information we collect

We do not require account creation, and we do not collect personal information such as your name or email address. Standard technical data may be collected automatically, including:

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (country/region level)

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies for basic site analytics, to understand traffic patterns and improve the site. If third-party advertising is served on this site, ad providers may set their own cookies to serve relevant ads; you can control this through your browser settings or the ad provider's opt-out tools.

Third-party data

Price data displayed on this site is sourced from CoinGecko's public market API. News headlines are sourced from third-party publications and linked back to their original sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Aggregated, non-identifying traffic data may be shared with analytics or advertising partners as part of standard ad-serving operations.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
